Hobbies

Music

I won't lie and say that I love all kinds of music genres, but I will say that I love music from many different genres. From Tyler the Creator and Kanye West to Asian Kung-Fu Generation and Unison Square Garden, I would say my music taste varies a bit. Currently I know how to play two instruments, the guitar and the piano. Since I was little I had always wanted to learn how to play the guitar from seeing other family members also playing the guitar and so around the age of 8 was when I first picked up the guitar to learn. Around the age of 15 or 16 in High School was when I first started to develop an interest in learning how to play the piano. I wasn't really inspired by anything or anyone to play the piano this time around, but I had always loved the sound of the piano notes and so I really wanted to give it a try and see if I could also play that instrument and ever since I've loved playing the piano.
